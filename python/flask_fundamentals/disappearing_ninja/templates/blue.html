Leonardo: The tactical, courageous leader and devoted student of his sensei Master Splinter, Leonardo wears a blue mask and wields two katana.
  • As the most conscientious of the four, he often bears the burden of responsibility for his brothers, which commonly leads to conflict with Raphael.
  • He is a charming character, with a super hero attitude, but his role as responsible leader was the source of contention to the other turtles in several episodes.
  • He had a brief romantic interest in a Japanese girl named Lotus Blossom.
  • Leonardo was named after the Italian polymath, painter, engineer, inventor, writer, anatomist, and sculptor, Leonardo da Vinci.
