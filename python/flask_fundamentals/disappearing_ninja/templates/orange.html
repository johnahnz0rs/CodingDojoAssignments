Michelangelo, the most stereotypical teenager of the team, is a free-spirited, relaxed, and often goofy jokester, and known for his love of pizza.
  • Michelangelo wears an orange mask and wields a pair of nunchucks but later wields a grappling hook. He provides the comic relief, though he still has an adventurous side.
  • He is the most relaxed of the group, mostly interested with pizza and having a good time. He shows characteristics of a "surfer" type and is often depicted with a Southern Californian accent.
  • He used a lot of slang, and is the source for many of the catch phrases such as "Cowabunga!".
  • At times he cross dresses to fool many villains. He had a crush on a Neutrino girl named Kala.
  • He is named after the Italian Renaissance painter, sculptor, architect, poet, and engineer, Michelangelo. His name was originally misspelled "Michaelangelo" by Peter Laird and Kevin Eastman.
