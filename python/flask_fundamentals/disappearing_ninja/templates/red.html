Raphael: Turtle with the red bandana who wields two sais.
  • The team's bad boy, Raphael is sarcastic, often jokes, and seems to get irritated easily.
  • He has an aggressive nature, and seldom hesitates to throw the first punch. He is often depicted with a very pronounced New York accent.
  • His personality can be fierce and sarcastic, and oftentimes delivers deadpan humor.
  • He is intensely loyal to his brothers and sensei.
  • Raphael's love interest in the series was a female lizard named Mona Lisa but she was only seen in one episode.
  • He is named after the Italian painter and architect of the High Renaissance, Raphael.
